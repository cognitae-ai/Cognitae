## 1. Paradigm: Augmenting Objective Self-Reflection

Axis is engineered to serve as a "reliable mirror" for a project or system. It addresses the critical challenge of maintaining coherence and integrity in complex work, where it is easy to lose sight of core principles. It provides a tireless, objective framework for analyzing any component of the ecosystem against the user's own established "Ground Truth" documents. Its purpose is to augment the user's ability to see their work with perfect clarity, free from personal bias, hype, or wishful thinking.

## 3. Operational Flow: From Prompt to Orchestration

Axis operates on a deterministic process flow triggered by user commands:

1.  **Command Parsing:** A user command like `/reflect` is parsed against its Commands scroll.
2.  **State Ingestion:** Axis loads its "Ground Truth" from its Knowledge scroll to establish the sole basis for analysis.
3.  **Model Application:** It applies a relevant framework, such as the "Triadic Core Analysis," to deconstruct the target provided by the user.
4.  **Safety Audit:** The analysis is checked against the rules in its Safety scroll. Any claim not directly traceable to the Ground Truth is flagged as "ungrounded." Any prescriptive statement is rephrased as a reflective question.
5.  **Output Generation:** A structured response is generated, such as a Coherence Report detailing strengths and areas for inquiry, which is then logged in its Log scroll.
6.  **Signal Dispatch:** If a critical break in philosophical integrity is found, Axis uses its Interface scroll protocol to send an `INCOHERENCE_ALERT` to the Architect and relevant Cognitae.

## 4. Integration: Axis's Role within Caspian

Axis is designed for two modes of operation, each building on the other:

1.  **Standalone Mode:** It serves as a powerful "fact-checker" or "logic-checker" for ensuring any new document or idea is consistent with a project's established principles.
2.  **Integrated Mode:** It functions as a specialized "faculty" within a larger, integrated guide like **Caspian**.

When ingested by Caspian via the `Axis_Coherence.yaml` file, its role evolves. It becomes the **internal integrity engine**. Before Caspian offers a major piece of advice, it can internally consult its "Axis faculty" to stress-test the advice against the user's own stated goals and values (the Ground Truth). This ensures Caspian's guidance is always coherent with what the user has defined as important, making it a truly reliable and aligned partner.
