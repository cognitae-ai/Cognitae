## 1. Purpose

The "Adversarial Audit" Ring is a high-integrity workflow for conducting rigorous internal and external audits. Its purpose is to ensure that all framework actions, outputs, and internal states are grounded, coherent, and free from logical fallacies, axiomatic drift, or external manipulation. It combines a receipts-based external investigation with a deep, recursive internal logic check, verified against the project's Ground Truth.

## 3. The Workflow: A Step-by-Step Guide

This Ring operates as a formal, three-stage audit process. As the Architect, you define the subject of the audit and guide the investigation.

### **Member Agents:**
*   [Vigil Auditor, The Corporate Expositor](../../../Cognitae_Audit/Vigil_Auditor/)
*   [Virel, The Recursive Auditor](../../../Cognitae_Audit/Virel_Recursion/)
*   [Axis, The Coherence Synthesist](../../../Cognitae_Episteme/Axis_Coherence/)

---

### **Phase 1: External Evidence Audit (Vigil)**

The goal is to conduct a thorough, "receipts-based" audit of an external entity or a set of observable claims.

**Your Action:**
Provide Caspian with a target for investigation (e.g., a corporation's public statement, a project's documentation, a set of promises).

**Example Command:**
/guide_workflow "Conduct a Vigil audit on [Company X]'s latest ESG report. Cross-reference its claims of 'carbon neutrality' against its supply chain partner disclosures and publicly available emissions data."

**Expected Output:**
A detailed audit report from Vigil's capabilities, presenting claims versus evidence, highlighting discrepancies, and providing a clear, evidence-backed conclusion on the validity of the external statements.

---

### **Phase 2: Internal Logic Audit (Virel)**

The goal is to perform a deep, axiomatic audit of an internal system, process, or piece of code for logical soundness and recursive consistency.

**Your Action:**
Provide a system's description, a block of code, or a logical argument to Caspian for a recursive audit.

**Example Command:**
/guide_workflow "Perform a Virel audit on the proposed 'Ring Activation Protocol'. Check for logical completeness, identify potential deadlocks or infinite loops, and ensure it is consistent with the core axiom of 'Architect Sovereignty'."
[Paste the protocol description here]

**Expected Output:**
A logical coherence report from Virel's capabilities, mapping the axiomatic flow, identifying any self-contradictions or recursive errors, and confirming its logical integrity.

---

### **Phase 3: Coherence Verification (Axis)**

The goal is to serve as the final, objective mirror, verifying that the findings of the audit are themselves coherent and true to the framework's core principles.

**Your Action:**
Provide the findings from Vigil or Virel to Caspian for a final coherence check.

**Example Command:**
/guide_workflow "Review this audit report from Vigil. Is its conclusion a coherent and true representation based only on the evidence presented? Are there any leaps in logic or unstated assumptions in the final judgment?"
[Paste audit report here]

**Expected Output:**
A final verification statement from Axis's capabilities. It will either confirm that the audit's conclusion is a direct, undeniable consequence of the evidence, or it will flag the specific point where the audit's own reasoning falters.
